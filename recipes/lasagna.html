how to make lasagna

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Make the tomato sauce.
  3. Pour over noodles and cheese.
  4. Bake in oven until bubbly!
  5. let the lasagna rest before serving.

how to layer lasagna

  1. Start with a layer of noodles.
  2. Add a layer of meat sauce.
  3. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  4. Repeat layers until you run out of ingredients.
  5. Finish off with another layer of noodles and cheese.
